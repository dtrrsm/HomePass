FAQ

What most people ask before using HomePass.

Do you replace the home inspector?
No. You still hire your own inspector. HomePass sits on top of that report and translates it into a clear, investor-style breakdown you can actually use.
Can I use this with an inspection I already did?
Yes. As long as you have the PDF or a link to the report, we can process it, even if your inspection was done weeks ago.
How long does it take to get my report?
Typical turnaround is under 24 hours for single reports and under 48 hours for multi-property packs. These targets are adjustable based on how you run the service.
What if I decide not to move forward on the property?
The report still pays off. You’ve avoided a bad deal and you can reuse the insights when evaluating the next property: what you’re willing to accept, what you’re not, and what certain issues really cost.
